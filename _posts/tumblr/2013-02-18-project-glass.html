Ever since this Google Glasses has been product I’ve kept a close eye on. I love the idea that all of and more than the functions of our mobile devices could be promoted to handsfree.

Google Glasses is handsfree in the purest sense but does’t loose out - it’s hands free by design, from the ground up - not just an afterthought or add-on. And in a totally new form. I think it has the potential to give us back what smartphones took away: our hands.

The idea that we no longer need use our hands to perform these actions is not a new one, however any implementation has never come this far before. It has the potential to be the biggest revolution since the mobile phone. It’s been well publicized and there have recently been the first developer sessions - apparently creating many new ways to use the product.

I’m most looking forward to the chance to take photos without fumbling for my phone/camera. I run in some lovely places but rarely take my phone. Google Glasses would solve this, allowing me to record the sight without interrupting the run. I’m not sure how it would work in terms of storing them but I’m sure it would be able to look after them while out of reception.

I think integration with phone and smart watches is important too. Like leave the glasses out for a self timer and take the photo with a phone. Or any way to reduce the amount of loud voice commands in public.

I really hope it fills out and that more details come soon - it’s going to be big.
